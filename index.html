About US

LimaT.sa is committed in proactively engaging communities along critical arterial routes (regional and national), understanding their challenges that are a cause for disputes in order to proactively advise the clients on the best arterial routes to take for their fleet with minimal disruption to the schedule.

Objectives

  • Effectively and purposefully engage with communities, private and governmental stakeholders on socioeconomic issues that have a potential to impact negatively on the client's operations.
  • Create an effective network of subject matter experts in communities to foster rapid communication and advice.
  • Strive for development and harmony within communities.
  • Effectively communicate with clients and provide advise on matters that seek to undermine or disrupt client's operations.

Benefits

  • Understanding social dynamics that negatively impact on the clients delivery of services.
  • Obtain first hand and usable information that will advise the client on the cause of action to embark upon.
  • Safety of the work force and the protection of fleet from injury and vandalism.
